About Page

As a fan of art, I wanted to create a website that allows people to explore and learn more about art from the comfort of their home.

With that in mind, I embarked on a journey to digitalize the Metropolitan Museum of Art's artwork and visitors to learn more.

The Home Page greets the users with 6 random artwork that introduces the people to the Met.

The Explore Page allows users to explore the MET's departments, and offers a glimpse of the art avaialble there.

The Search Page gives the users the opportunity to search for the art, artist, or keyword that they would like to explore.

The Saved Art page displays the art the users have found interesting, as a journal for their exploration.

INST377 Final Project using MET and Supabase API
